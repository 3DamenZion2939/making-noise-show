About The Making Noise Show

Speaking Truth to Power

Welcome to The Making Noise Show — a weekly podcast that spreads the Gospel everywhere while adding a dash of political satire and insightful commentary on current events. Hosted by Diego and team, the show blends Biblical scripture with a sharp eye on the news cycle, giving listeners a unique perspective on faith, culture, and politics.

With a mission to speak truth to power, we dive into topics that matter — from everyday challenges to headline news — all through the lens of scripture and hope. Whether you’re a lifelong believer or just curious about how faith and society intersect, we invite you to join us each week for thought-provoking discussions and a healthy dose of humor.

Let’s make some noise together!
